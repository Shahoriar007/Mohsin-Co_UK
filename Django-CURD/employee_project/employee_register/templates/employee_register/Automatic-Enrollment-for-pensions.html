Automatic Enrolment for Pension

Whether you work full time or part-time in the UK and earn more than £10,000 in a tax year, your employer will have to enroll in work-place pension.

If you are earning less than £10,000 but more than £6,240 you can still join the pension scheme.

Why it is beneficial for you to join pension scheme?

1. You will be entitled to tax saving on the contribution you are making towards your pension.

2. You will be able to build up saving for your future to provide you with a source of an income when you choose to retire.

At Mohsin & co. we can help you with eligibility requirement guidance and advice regarding pension scheme so that you can be worry free with your future.
