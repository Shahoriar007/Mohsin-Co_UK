Payroll & RTI submission

It can be time-consuming and complicated to run your own payroll, particularly with the ever growing complexity of taxes, jobs and pension legislation. The pressure on companies to comply with these issues is rising and non-compliance can lead to significant penalties. Our service is extensive and confidential and involves:

1. Payslips created by computers.

2. PAYE administration, National Statutory and insurance payments.

3. Completion of statutory forms and coping with HMRC forms and correspondence (including year end returns).

4. Summaries of staff expenses and analysis Incentive programs, bonuses and ex-gratia termination payments administration.

5. Assistance with regulations on pensions.

6. Real Time Information ( RTI) returns to HMRC On (or before) the first pay day, the RTI submission is due. E.g. If you pay your workers on a Friday every week, the first RTI return should be sent to staff on or before the first Friday that you make payments. On the last day of the calendar month, if you pay workers regularly, then the first RTI return should be submitted on or before the last day of that month.

7. We have advanced tools that can help monitor and evaluate the types of submissions that are due at any given moment in time.
