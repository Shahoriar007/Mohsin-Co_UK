Business Start-ups Advisory

From developing a strategic strategy to building partnerships with financial institutions, we deliver a broad range of business consulting services. We will support if you are just starting up or already set up to catapult your business success. Via specific consultation, Mohsin & Co. offers to set everything for you, enabling you to concentrate your focus and valuable time for the company.

Many individuals who start up in business because they have a brilliant business idea, but the financial aspects of running the business can also be overwhelming for the entrepreneur. We have your back and support you by analyzing your ideas and helping your company to be financially viable and successful. Be stress free.

Our works comprise of

1. Deciding on the most acceptable framework for your company (Sole Trader, Partnership, Limited Liability Partnership or Limited Company)

2. To show and illustrate the financial feasibility of your business proposals to financial institutions or your potential investors, prepare a business plan, cash flow estimate, budget and trading forecast.

3. Evaluate your criteria for finance and access the most suitable source.

4. Help you build a partnership through our well-established communication network with banks, solicitors and other professionals.

5. Conduct the registration process with the house of the company and HMRC.

6. Establish an internal system of accounting that complies with legislative requirements.
